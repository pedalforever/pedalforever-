1978 Roger DeCoster – World Champion Build

From the 617 to the 603 — rescued just outside of Boston, Massachusetts.
This DeCoster came back to Nashua and was refurbished 110% in-house using its original parts in Nashua, New Hampshire.

Right down to its green Roger DeCoster #1 World Champion number plate.
This build features Skyway Tuff II wheels, Tange forks, and Race Inc. tall bars in anodized blue.

Out back — MX-1000 rear brake caliper paired with a Dia-Compe MX brake lever.
Drive setup includes a Takagi chromoly 175x24 crankset, CAM USA sprocket, and Wellgo pedals.

Cockpit and finishing details include ACS BMX stem, AME grips, ACS seat clamp, and a GT BMX seat.
Set off with a Tuff Neck USA laid-back seat post and chain tensioners.

Still wearing its original nickel plating.
This one’s not just restored — it’s preserved.

This bike’s a ripper.
