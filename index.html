Meet the Pedal Forever Factory Racing Team

One guy screws loose. One tightens loose screws. And the third still thinks he is the fastest guy at the track because of something that happened in 1998. Together, they are the Pedal Forever Factory Racing Team — a garage-built BMX disaster held together by chrome, caffeine, old stories, bad ideas, and just enough mechanical knowledge to remain legally operational.
