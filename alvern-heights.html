ALVERN HEIGHTS

Built on BMX, bad decisions, pizza grease, and surviving another night.

PF Pizza

The unofficial headquarters of Alvern Heights. Known for late-night deliveries, BMX arguments, and grease stains older than most riders.

Griffin's Pedal Forever

Restoration shop, museum, survivor sanctuary, and accidental gathering place for every BMX disaster in town.

Railside Drive

Fast backroad route through town. Minimal police presence. Maximum questionable decisions.

The Sandpits

Illegal jumps. Burnouts. Broken frames. Some of the best stories in Alvern started here.

Industrial District

Rusted warehouses, hidden garages, scrap piles, and people who somehow always know where rare BMX parts are.

Alvern Raceway

Home of the city's BMX glory days. Every rider in town thinks they could've gone pro here.

Alvern Municipal Police

Constantly attempting to stop Pinchbolt Pete. Constantly failing.

Alvern General

The hospital responsible for reattaching half the riders in this city at least once.
