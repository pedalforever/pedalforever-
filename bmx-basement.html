Basement Artifact #001

The Miracle Boy Left His Mark

Six minutes from home. Two miles from the basement. Buried in the dry back room of an old garage, this signed Dave Mirra Haro had been sitting untouched for nearly 26 years.

It did not show up polished, staged, or ready for a museum. It surfaced the way real survivor bikes do — dusty, honest, half-forgotten, and still carrying the kind of mark that makes your stomach drop when you realize what you are looking at.

Autographed by The Miracle Boy himself, the frame escaped the usual fate: stripped, repainted, parted out, or lost forever.

The full bike is here. It is slowly working its way back together piece by piece, era correct at all costs.

“Some people find bikes. Some people uncover history.”
