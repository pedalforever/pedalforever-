Inside The Booth

At Pedal Forever, preserving original paint will always come first. A bike only leaves the factory one time wearing its original finish, and once that history is stripped away, it can never truly be replaced.

That said, not every bike survives untouched. Some arrive already stripped. Some were repainted decades ago. Some have rust too deep to ignore. Some deserve another chance. That’s where The Booth comes in.

Every frame gets evaluated individually. Every dent gets assessed. Every imperfection tells part of the story. Nothing gets buried blindly under filler and shiny paint just for the sake of appearance. The goal has never been perfection. The goal is respect.

This room is where the decisions happen. Color matching. Masking lines. Primer stages. Clear coat flow. Metal preparation. Funkadelic colorways. Survivor touch-ups. Factory-inspired restorations. Sometimes restraint matters more than repainting the entire bike. Sometimes saving what remains is the real art form.

The Booth is equal parts restoration lab, archive room, and bad decision factory. This is where Pedal Forever bikes either stay alive... or come back to life.

“Some bikes need restoration. Some only need respect.”
